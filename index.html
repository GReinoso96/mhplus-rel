Monster Hunter 1J Plus

Version 3.0

Monster Hunter 1J Plus is an improvement patch for the original Japanese version of Monster Hunter on the PS2, it supports both the Japanese original and the V8 English Patch.

Changelog

  • All features are now combined into one patch, they can be toggled in the Main Menu->Options screen.
  • Anamorphic Widescreeen Mode.
    • Only affects quest HUD and field of view.
  • Portable and Analog control schemes.
  • Victory Cam skip (Optional).
  • Slotted Village Quest system (Optional).
  • Elemental display in Blademaster weapon infoboxes (Mandatory).
