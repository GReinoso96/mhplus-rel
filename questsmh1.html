1J Plus Slotted Quest List

3 Star Quests

Slot 1 is either The Formidable Velocidrome or Velocidrome Redux

Slot 2 is always Liver of Legend

Slot 3 is either Jungle Menace or Slay the Yian Kut-Ku!

Slot 4 is always Slay the Velociprey

Slot 5 is either The Mushroom Hunt or Wyvern Egg Acquisition!

4 Star Quests

Slot 1 is either Slay the Velociprey Pack, Bring Me Eggs! or Find the Wyvern Eggs!

Slot 2 is always Catch a Yian Kut-Ku

Slot 3 is either The Land Shark or Mushroom Delivery!

Slot 4 is either Attack of the Giant Bugs! or Poisonous Fangs

Slot 5 is either Slay the Genprey! or Slay the Gendrome!

5 Star Quests

Slot 1 is either Fang of the Iodrome or Horn of the Monoblos (After it has been cleared as an Urgent)

Slot 2 is either Attack of the Rathalos or Catch a Rathalos

Slot 3 is either The Genprey Pack or Gendrome Redux!

Slot 4 is either Giant Bug Attack or Velocidrome Eradication

Slot 5 is either Deliver 3 Wyvern Eggs or The Velociprey Pack!
