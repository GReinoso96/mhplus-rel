Monster Hunter 2 Plus

Improvement patches for Monster Hunter 2.

Features

  • Smithy Speedup: Smithy no longer plays an unskippable animation.
  • Extra Item Boxes:
    • Jumbo (Full Box): Next to the item shop felyne.
    • Kokoto (Full Box): Next to the door to your house.
    • Foundry (Full Box): To the left of the smithy.
    • City Gate (Limited Box): To the left of the dragon observatory.
    • Dundorma Square (Limited Box): Next to the combiner.
    • Dundorma Tavern (Limited Box): Top left corner.
    • Dundorma Elder Hall (Limited Box): Left of the quest counter.
  • Shipwright Speedup: When Master Shipwright asks if you want to travel, the default option will now be Yes
  • PSP Controls: Game controls similar to later games in the series.
